Who We Are?

Meet Our CEO - Petya

With over 15 years of experience in animal welfare, Petya has dedicated his life to creating a safe haven for animals in need. Under his leadership, our shelter has rescued and rehomed more than 5,000 animals.

Our animal shelter is committed to providing care, love, and shelter to abandoned and rescued animals. We believe every animal deserves a chance at a happy life.

  • Over 5,000 animals rescued since 2008
  • 24/7 veterinary care facility
  • Network of 1,000+ volunteer families
  • 100% transparency in fund utilization
